The exam consists of three sections: Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Each section contains 25 questions, with 20 multiple-choice questions (MCQs) and 5 numerical value questions. Focusing on quality over quantity ensures deep understanding.</span></p><p dir="ltr"><b><strong>3. Mock Tests</strong></b><span>: Taking regular mock tests to simulate exam conditions, identify weak areas, and improve time management.</span></p><p dir="ltr"><u><b><strong class="GFGEditorTheme__textUnderline">Resources</strong></b></u></p><p dir="ltr"><b><strong>1.
